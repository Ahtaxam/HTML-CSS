Ingredients need to make Dish

  • Measuring cup
  • Water
  • Saucepan with lid
  • Salt
  • Rice
  • Wooden Spoon
  • Fork

Detailed step to make dish

  1. Boil water and add salt
    • add 3/4 cup of water in fresh rice
    • mix 1 teaspoon salt in water
  2. Pour in Rice
    • add rice into boiling water
  3. Stir once or just enough to seperate the rice
    • use wooden spoon to seperate ant clumps
  4. Cover the pot and simmer
    • Be sure lid fit tightly
    • Turn down heat for 5 minutes
  5. flup rice with a fork
